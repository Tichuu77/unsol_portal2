DOZEE - The Only Early Warning System

Highlight

Dozee is India's 1st Contactless Remote Patient Monitoring (RPM) and Early Warning System (EWS). It uses Ballistocardiography and AI-powered algorithms to convert micro-vibrations from heartbeats, respiration, and body movements into bio-markers, aiding in monitoring patient vitals trends.

Application Benefit
  • Improves Nursing Quality
  • Reduces High Nursing Workload and Manual Errors
Key Vitals Monitored

Eliminate manual errors with AI-Based Contactless RPM and EWS. Monitor key vitals with timely alerts for enhanced patient safety:

  • Heart Rate, Respiration Rate, Blood Pressure
  • SpO2, Pulse Rate, ECG, Temperature

Science Behind Dozee

BALLISTOCARDIOGRAPHY

The science of collecting & analyzing micro-vibrations

These vibrations are produced with every heartbeat, inhalations, exhalations, muscle twitches, snoring, and body movements.

Placed under the mattress, Dozee even captures feeble vibrations like the opening and closing of Mitral and Aortic valves inside the heart.

DEEP LEARNING

These vibrations are too noisy to make any sense of.

That’s where Dozee’s proprietary Advanced AI algorithms step in to make it one of the most precise tools. Literally helping it to find a needle in a haystack!

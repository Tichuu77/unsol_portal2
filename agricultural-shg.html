Internal Design – Barrier Concept

In a barrier concept laundry, the laundry processing area is divided as per below:

Contaminated area (1/3 of the surface)
  • Arrival of the dirty laundry
  • Trolley storage to disinfect
  • Sorting dirty laundry
  • Washer extractors loading
  • Sanitary cloakroom
  • Room between dirty and clean
  • Technical room
  • Chemical storage
Clean area (2/3 of the surface)
  • Washer extractors unloading
  • Tumbler dryers
  • Finishing
  • Folding + wrapping
  • Sorting
  • Storage
  • Expeditions

SurroundScope

SURROUNDSCOPE is a 270-Degree pioneering laparoscopic system, enabling surgeons to operate in a new, safer, and more efficient way with clear visualization, eliminating fog/smoke.

Allows you to keep the wide field of view usually missing in laparoscopy. The 270-degree view provides wide cavity visualization.
